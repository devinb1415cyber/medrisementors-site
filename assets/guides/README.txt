  mcat-study-guide-preview.pdf              (live — MCAT Study Guide preview)

To turn a "Coming soon" card into a live download, open resources.html, find that
guide in the guides grid rendered on the page, and change the card's button from the
"Coming soon" span to a Download link pointing at your PDF (see the MCAT cards for an
example).
