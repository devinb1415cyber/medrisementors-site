Meet MedRise Mentors.

A closer look at who we are, why we exist, and how peer mentorship helps students rise into medicine.

Why we exist

Talent is everywhere. The map to medicine isn't.

The path into medicine rewards insider knowledge — which experiences count, how to read an application cycle, what a competitive story looks like. Students without connections in medicine too often navigate it blind, piecing together conflicting advice from forums like Reddit and Student Doctor Network.

MedRise Mentors removes those barriers with the people best positioned to help: medical students who walked the same road a few years earlier, and remember exactly how it felt.

No student should have to figure this journey out alone.

What we do

Two ways we help students rise.

MedRise Mentors delivers engaging presentations to schools and pairs students one-on-one with medical-student mentors — backed by free resources for every step.

Engaging presentations

We visit high schools and universities to demystify the premed path — personal statements, extracurriculars, interviews, and what a competitive application really takes.

1:1 longitudinal mentorship

Every mentee is paired with a medical-student mentor for ongoing, personal guidance through the whole journey — not a one-time chat.

Application guidance

From building your extracurricular profile to interview prep and a compelling personal statement — practical help where it counts most.

Free resources

A growing library of vetted guides for premeds and medical students — open to everyone, at no cost.

A national mentor network

48 medical-student mentors from institutions across the country, so students from all backgrounds find guidance that fits their journey.

Mentor-matching platform Coming soon

We're building a platform to make matching, shared goals, and roadmaps even easier — launching soon.

How mentorship works

From first question to white coat.

Four steps to a mentor who's been there — and stays with you through the whole journey.

01

Apply in minutes

Tell us where you are — first-year undecided, gap-year applicant, or anywhere between. A short form is all it takes to start.

02

Get matched with intention

We pair you with a medical-student mentor by goals, background, and interests — someone who was recently in your seat.

03

Meet your mentor

Start with a conversation. Ask the questions the forums can't answer, and map out your next steps together.

04

Rise, together

Longitudinal support through coursework, the MCAT, applications, and interviews — all the way to the acceptance letter.

Our framework

Mentorship with a method: RISE

Every MedRise Mentors match runs on the same four commitments — the difference between a name on a list and a mentor who shows up.

R
Relationships

Peer mentor connections built on trust, not transactions — mentors who genuinely remember the climb.

I
Insight

Lived, current guidance from students inside the process today — not advice from a decade ago.

S
Support

Structured, longitudinal mentorship that stays with you between milestones.

E
Empowerment

The confidence and clarity to own your path — and one day, to reach back and pull someone up.
