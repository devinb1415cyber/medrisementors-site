Where it goes

See the impact of your gift.

We're an all-volunteer nonprofit — so 100% of your gift goes directly to a student, never to salaries, overhead, or hidden admin fees. Every dollar helps make medicine more inclusive for first-generation and underserved students.

$500

Covers a student's MCAT registration and a couple of full-length practice tests.

$1,000

Covers a large portion of a student's primary application — the first real gate into medical school.

$2,000

Covers a full round of a student's secondary applications, so cost never decides where they apply.

$3,000

Covers interview season — flights, hotels, and a suit — for a student who earned the invite but can't afford the trip.
