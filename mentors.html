The mentors corner

Meet the mentors walking just ahead.

Our mentors are carefully selected medical students who speak at universities and high schools and take on individual mentee pairings — offering longitudinal support throughout the premed journey, all at no cost, so access to mentorship is never a barrier to pursuing medicine.

Why our mentors

Guidance that's relevant to your journey.

Carefully selected

Medical students with strong academic foundations and diverse backgrounds, each bringing a unique perspective.

Nationwide reach

Located across the United States and representing a wide range of institutions, so every student finds a relevant fit.

Trained to guide

On everything from the premed pathway and competitive applications to volunteering, interviews, and personal statements.
